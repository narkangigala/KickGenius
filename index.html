Sneakers Collection

Nike Air Max 90

The Nike Air Max 90 is a classic sneaker known for its iconic design and comfortable cushioning. It features a leather and mesh upper, visible Air unit in the heel, and a rubber outsole for traction. Available in various colorways.

Adidas Superstar

The Adidas Superstar is a legendary sneaker that has been popular since the 1970s. It features a classic shell toe design, leather upper, and rubber cupsole. The Superstar is known for its versatile style and has become an iconic sneaker in streetwear culture.

New Balance 574

The New Balance 574 is a retro-inspired sneaker with a timeless design. It features a suede and mesh upper, ENCAP midsole technology for cushioning and support, and a durable rubber outsole. The 574 offers both comfort and style for everyday wear.
